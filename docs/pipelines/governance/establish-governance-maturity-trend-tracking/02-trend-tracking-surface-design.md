# Trend Tracking Surface Design

## Canonical File

Pipeline 151 establishes:

`docs/governance/governance-maturity-history.md`

## Surface Role Separation

- `docs/governance/governance-maturity-scorecard.md`
  - canonical current-state maturity surface
- `docs/governance/governance-maturity-history.md`
  - canonical temporal maturity surface

## Required Sections

The history surface is initialized with these stable sections:

- Current Reading
- Historical Record
- Trend Interpretation Rules
- Known Limitations
- Change Recording Discipline

## Design Outcome

The trend surface extends maturity reporting with temporal structure while
preserving the scorecard as the source of current maturity truth.
