# Trend Interpretation Boundaries

## Allowed Trend Classes

- `newly established`
- `improving`
- `unchanged`
- `regressing`
- `recalibrated with explanation`

## Bounded Meaning

- `newly established`
  - first canonical maturity observation only
- `improving`
  - current score is greater than prior score
- `unchanged`
  - current score equals prior score
- `regressing`
  - current score is lower than prior score
- `recalibrated with explanation`
  - score movement or interpretation changes because a governance pipeline
    formally changed the scoring basis and documented why

## Prohibited Claims

The trend surface must not claim:

- predictive future maturity movement
- irreversible governance gains
- causal explanations not supported by explicit evidence
- trend direction without a recorded score basis
