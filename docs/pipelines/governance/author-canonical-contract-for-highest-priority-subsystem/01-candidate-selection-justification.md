# Candidate Selection Justification

## Source Artifacts Consulted

- `docs/governance/contract-discovery-ledger.md`
- `docs/pipelines/governance/discover-contract-candidates-and-authority-surfaces/05-contract-candidate-matrix.md`
- `docs/pipelines/governance/discover-contract-candidates-and-authority-surfaces/08-contract-discovery-recommendations.md`
- `docs/pipelines/governance/audit-repository-governance-readiness/04-pipeline-registry-integrity.md`

## Ranking Basis

- governance risk
- immediate auditability value
- centrality to active repository operations
- narrow, evidence-based scope suitable for first canonical contract

## Selection Decision

The `Pipeline Registry Integrity Contract` is the highest-priority subsystem contract because:

- the registry is already a named governance surface
- the constitution already requires registration for active pipelines
- prior audit evidence showed the registry was incomplete relative to active practice
- the contract can be authored without inventing absent product-domain systems

## Deferred Candidates

- `Contract Root Stewardship Contract`
- `Pipeline Artifact Interpretation Contract`
- `Pipeline Orchestration Contract`

These remain important, but they are deferred because registry integrity is the most immediate operational gap.
