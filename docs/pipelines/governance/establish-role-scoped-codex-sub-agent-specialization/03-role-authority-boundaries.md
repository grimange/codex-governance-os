# Role Authority Boundaries

All Layer 4 roles remain subordinate to:

- Layer 0 doctrine and safety canons
- Layer 1 interpretation canon
- Layer 2 execution surfaces
- Layer 3 Codex rules canon

Key authority limits recorded by the role model:

- no role may self-authorize beyond lower-layer boundaries
- no role may replace governed routing with convenience behavior
- verifier and reporter responsibilities remain distinct
- specialization does not imply stronger mutation authority
- specialization does not imply automatic delegation or orchestration
