# Evidence Classification Model

The implementation classifies explicit evidence into the bounded harness
families defined by the continuity doctrine:

- `explicit_session_predecessor_reference`
- `governance_artifact_continuation`
- `artifact_chain_evidence`
- `canonical_continuation_marker`

Current scenario coverage exercises:

- verified continuity through predecessor linkage plus governance artifact
  continuation
- weak continuity through one governed artifact continuation link
- no continuity through absence of admissible evidence
- boundary violation through prohibited implicit reasoning with no admissible
  bridge evidence

Unknown evidence strings do not elevate continuity strength.
