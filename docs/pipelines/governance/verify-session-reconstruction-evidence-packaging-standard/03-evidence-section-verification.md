# Evidence Section Verification

The packaging canon preserves the required evidence sections:

- source session evidence
- governance doctrine citations
- pipeline artifact references
- reconstruction assumptions
- restriction record

Verification findings:

- each required section is named explicitly in the canon
- source-session evidence remains distinct from doctrine and artifact citation
  sections
- assumptions remain their own explicit section when inference is required
- restrictions remain their own explicit section instead of being absorbed into
  narrative summary

## Classification

- required evidence sections present: `VERIFIED`
- assumption section preserved: `VERIFIED`
- restriction section preserved: `VERIFIED`
- section drift observed: `NOT OBSERVED`
