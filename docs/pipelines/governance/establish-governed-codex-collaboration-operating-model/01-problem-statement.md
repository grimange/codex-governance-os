# Problem Statement

Pipelines `087` and `088` established and verified the bounded Layer 4 Codex
role model, but the repository still lacked one canonical surface explaining
how those roles collaborate during governed execution.

Without that collaboration model:

- role handoffs remain partially implicit
- restrictions risk being lost between planning, integration, verification,
  and reporting
- authority preservation across collaboration steps remains under-specified
- later lanes could overclaim orchestration that the repository does not prove

Pipeline `089` closes that gap by establishing a bounded Layer 5 collaboration
operating model.
