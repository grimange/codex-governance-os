# Contract Authority Surfaces

The drift detector treats these surfaces as alignment authorities:

- `docs/contracts/universal-template-composition-contract.md`
- `tools/templates/composition_contract.py`
- manifest inventory under `docs/codex/templates/manifests/`
- `tools/governance/template_scaffold.py`
- `tools/templates/list_templates.py`

The contract document is the human-readable certified matrix.

The runtime engine remains the executable authority.

Drift detection verifies that both remain aligned and that manifest and tooling surfaces do not imply broader support than the certified contract allows.
