# Promotion Decision

Decision: PROMOTE_WITH_OBSERVATIONS

## Basis

- The template demonstrates strong doctrine, skill, pipeline, and bootstrap compatibility across backend, frontend, CLI, infrastructure, library, and monorepo archetypes.
- No material stack-specific bias was found in the canonical governance surfaces.
- The primary weaknesses are onboarding and publication clarity, not architectural incompatibility.

## Observations

- A top-level adoption entrypoint is still missing.
- Smaller repositories may need clearer lightweight adoption guidance so the template feels proportional at first use.
