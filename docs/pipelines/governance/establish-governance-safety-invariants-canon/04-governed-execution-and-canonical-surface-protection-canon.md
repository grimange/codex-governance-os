- [governance-safety-invariants-canon.md](/home/ramjf/python-projects/codex-governance-os/docs/governance/governance-safety-invariants-canon.md)

The doctrine now makes the following safety rules explicit:

- structural governance mutation requires governed execution
- convenience does not justify governance bypass
- governance changes must not self-authorize through lower-order edits
- canonical governance truth surfaces must remain protected
- canonical truth must not be changed through summary-only restatement
- split-authority drift must be reconciled explicitly rather than tolerated

These rules centralize protections that were previously distributed across
architecture doctrine, evolution doctrine, and subsystem contracts.
