# Authority Precedence And Semantic Safety Canon

The canon also centralizes the final two invariant classes:

- authority precedence invariants
  - canonical repository truth outranks narrative, convenience reasoning, and
    unsupported AI inference
- semantic safety invariants
  - editing, normalization, or cleanup must not silently change governance
    meaning

The doctrine makes explicit that unsupported, blocked, or partial states must
not be hidden behind neutral wording, and that normalization must block or use a
higher-authority lane when it would change governance meaning.
